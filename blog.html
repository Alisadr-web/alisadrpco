Why I Chose ICT

ICT fits my way of thinking and passion for problem solving. I enjoy coding and exploring how technology shapes our future. ICT helps simplify complex problems and offers faster, smarter solutions for daily challenges.

My Programming Experience

I started with Python and SQL. Initially, it was challenging, but building small projects helped me improve step by step. I am focused on backend development and enjoy learning new frameworks and tools to enhance my skills. My goal is to become a competent programmer capable of developing scalable applications.

First Feedback and Learnings

My first assignment received feedback about writing cleaner code and organizing structure effectively. The teaching assistants at HZ University provided valuable tips to improve design, maintainability, and website quality. This feedback motivated me to keep refining my skills and approach each project with better planning.

Useful Tips for ICT Students

  • Practice coding daily to strengthen logic and problem-solving skills.
  • Work on personal projects to apply concepts learned in class.
  • Explore open-source contributions to gain real-world experience.
  • Learn both frontend and backend fundamentals to become a versatile developer.
  • Keep a study journal to track progress, challenges, and solutions.
